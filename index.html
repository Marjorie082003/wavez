Amazing Ocean Facts

71% of Earth

The ocean covers about 71% of the Earth's surface and contains 97% of the planet's water.

80% of Life

Approximately 80% of all life on Earth is found in the ocean, much of it still undiscovered.

Deepest Point

The Mariana Trench is the deepest part of the ocean at nearly 11,000 meters (36,000 feet) deep.
